Eco efficiency (measures to minimize environmental impact through the reduction or elimination of waste from production processes) has become a goal for companies worldwide, with many realizing significant cost savings from such innovations. Peter Senge and Goran Carstedt see this development as laudable but suggest that simply adopting eco efficiency innovations could actually worsen environmental stresses in the future.
A
explain why a particular business strategy has been less successful than was once anticipated
B
explain why a particular business strategy has been less successful than was once anticipated
C
explain why a particular business strategy has been less successful than was once anticipated
D
explain why a particular business strategy has been less successful than was once anticipated
提交

正确答案：C


题目解析
非谓语动词：svo,doing结构中doing不能跨过逗号就近修饰名词，比如本题试图使用reducing直接修饰前面的名词 an event，不正确，所以A错误
搭配： so that和so as to紧紧放在一起表目的的。本题中的event无法有目的，所以CE错误。
引导词：此处event是动作发出者，使用which引导定语从句时，前面的介词应该是by而不是from，所以D错误。
平行结构：and前后平行，未划线and后面为名词，所以CD不平行错误。
